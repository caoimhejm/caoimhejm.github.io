Integrated Circuit

What is an Integrated Circuit?

An integrated circuit is a circuit designed to fit on a tiny chip, often called a chip, microchip, or IC. It can be a microcontroller, an amplifier, a Bluetooth module and more.

An integrated circuit is made up of lots of miniature versions of basic components like transistors, resistors, and capacitors. The chip's purpose depends on how the components have been connected inside. It can be a radio transmitter, a collection of logic gates, a microcontroller, or any other circuit you can think of.

How are Integrated Circuits made?

To make an integrated circuit from the very start, you use special software. In the software, you draw rectangles to build your circuit. Each rectangle stands for different materials and combining these in different ways can create different parts.
